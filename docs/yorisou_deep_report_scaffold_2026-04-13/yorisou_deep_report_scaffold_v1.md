## Structural Rule

The deep report expands the same persona structure rather than replacing it.

It can explain tension, hidden pattern, and growth direction, but it must not invent new persona logic.

## Hotspot Boundary Protection

### P09 / P25

- Why deep-report expansion is risky: Long-form writing can turn one-to-one reply temperature and group / weekend reply rhythm into the same “communication style” story.
- Structural levers that must stay distinct: Keep one-to-one temperature versus group / scene timing as separate axes.
- Paragraph shape to avoid: “この人は返事が早くて、相手にやさしくて、連絡もうまい”とひとまとめにする paragraph.
- Safeguard rule: Every report must name the context: LINE相手, 場, 週末, グループのどれで動くかを分ける。

### P11 / P12 / P26 / P29

- Why deep-report expansion is risky: All are low-energy recovery states; the report can easily flatten them into “疲れている / 休みたい” only.
- Structural levers that must stay distinct: Keep the time anchor and load anchor: solo quiet, night reserve, evening recovery, weekend downshift.
- Paragraph shape to avoid: “この人は休むのが上手です”で終わる paragraph.
- Safeguard rule: 必ず「いつ」「どの負荷で」回復するかを明記する。

### P13 / P20 / P21

- Why deep-report expansion is risky: Change tolerance, decision latency, and action threshold all sound like “flexible / decisive” if expanded loosely.
- Structural levers that must stay distinct: Keep the threshold verb: 着地, 線引き, まず動く.
- Paragraph shape to avoid: “変化に強く、決断も早いです”のように線引きを消す paragraph.
- Safeguard rule: 「待つ」「決める」「動く」のどれが先かを明文化する。

### P03 / P15 / P23

- Why deep-report expansion is risky: All involve load and switching. A deep report can easily reduce them to a generic “busy life” narrative.
- Structural levers that must stay distinct: Sequence / simultaneous load / load tuning must remain distinct.
- Paragraph shape to avoid: “毎日忙しくても頑張る人です”で止まる paragraph.
- Safeguard rule: 用事の種類と負荷の形を必ず分ける。

### P04 / P06 / P27

- Why deep-report expansion is risky: Shopping, tidying, and reward-reset all feel like “life reset” when expanded, which erases the real object being handled.
- Structural levers that must stay distinct: Basket / placement / reward-plus-cleanup must stay explicit.
- Paragraph shape to avoid: “生活を整えると気分も上がる”だけの paragraph.
- Safeguard rule: 何を整えるのかを先に書き、気分は後に置く。

### P07 / P24

- Why deep-report expansion is risky: Safety check and repeated confirmation will collapse into a generic cautiousness story if not separated.
- Structural levers that must stay distinct: Environment hazard check versus repeated safety confirmation.
- Paragraph shape to avoid: “慎重で安全意識が高いです”で同じにする paragraph.
- Safeguard rule: 危険そのものを見るのか、確認を重ねるのかを分ける。

### P18 / P31

- Why deep-report expansion is risky: Both are spacing and social rhythm. A long report can erase the one-to-one vs group scale difference.
- Structural levers that must stay distinct: One-to-one spacing versus room / group / community spacing.
- Paragraph shape to avoid: “人との距離感がうまいです”で完結する paragraph.
- Safeguard rule: 相手と場を同じ文で扱わず、スケールを固定して書く。
