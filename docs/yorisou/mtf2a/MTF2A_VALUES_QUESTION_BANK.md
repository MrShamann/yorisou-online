## Design rules honored (duplication & overlap review)

- **Side balance:** each pair's two base scenarios flip which dimension sits on A (e.g. Q01 anshin-A / Q22 anshin-B), damping position bias.
- **Genuine trade-offs:** both options are desirable in every item; no morally "correct" side; no obvious answer.
- **Scenario diversity:** 転職/住まい/週末/夜/学び/誘い/お金の余裕/新生活/趣味/教える立場 — no two items reuse a stem for the same pair (semantic-overlap pass in `MTF2A_STRUCTURED_REVIEW_RECORD.md` removed two near-duplicates during production).
- **Not alignable item-by-item with any external instrument** — situations and wording are YORISOU-authored; the pairwise-comparison abstract pattern is unprotectable common ground (see the originality register).
- **No exposure of** financial specifics, medical, legal, or intimate detail; the money item (Q25) is deliberately about 余裕のあるお金 with no amounts.
