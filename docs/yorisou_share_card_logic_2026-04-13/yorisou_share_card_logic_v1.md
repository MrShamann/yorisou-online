## Share-Card Operating Rules

- Share-card logic is one notch tighter than the teaser layer.
- Share-card lines must stay screenshot-capable, native Japanese, and one-read legible.
- Share-card copy may compress the hook, but may not erase the persona boundary.
- No line may rely on mythology names to make sense.
- No line may become generic “relatable life advice”.
- No line may sound like survey, counsel, corporate copy, or AI assistant copy.

## Share-Card Calibration Scale

- Japanese naturalness: 1-5
- emotional truth: 1-5
- entertainment value: 1-5
- screenshot/share potential: 1-5
- structural distinction retention: 1-5
- compression safety: 1-5

## Hotspot Compression Guardrails

### P09 / P25

- Why compression is risky: P09 is one-to-one reply temperature; P25 is group / weekend reply rhythm. Short share copy can flatten both into “返信が早い人” or “返事がやさしい人”.
- Key distinguishing lever: Retain the context axis: one-to-one temperature vs group/scene timing.
- Bad short line to avoid: 返事が早くてやさしい。
- Guardrail for recommended writing: Keep at least one context word in the line: LINE / 場 / 週末 / グループ.

### P11 / P12 / P26 / P29

- Why compression is risky: All are low-energy recovery or reduced-load states. Compression can collapse them into “疲れている人”.
- Key distinguishing lever: Retain the time anchor: solo quiet / night reserve / evening recovery / weekend downshift.
- Bad short line to avoid: 静かに休む人。
- Guardrail for recommended writing: Every line must keep the timing or load context visible.

### P13 / P20 / P21

- Why compression is risky: All touch change, decision, and action threshold. A short line can erase whether the person waits, decides, or moves first.
- Key distinguishing lever: Keep the threshold verb: 着地 / 線引き / まず動く.
- Bad short line to avoid: 変化に強い人。
- Guardrail for recommended writing: State the action boundary explicitly, not just “flexible” or “decisive”.

### P03 / P15 / P23

- Why compression is risky: All are load and switching related. Compression can make them all look like “忙しい人”.
- Key distinguishing lever: Retain the load shape: sequencing / simultaneous load / load tuning.
- Bad short line to avoid: 忙しいけど頑張る。
- Guardrail for recommended writing: Name the kind of load, not just the busyness.

### P04 / P06 / P27

- Why compression is risky: Shopping, tidying, and reward-reset all involve daily-life reset energy. Short copy can blur them into “生活を整える人”.
- Key distinguishing lever: Keep the primary object: shopping basket / placement / reward-plus-cleanup.
- Bad short line to avoid: 生活を整える人。
- Guardrail for recommended writing: Anchor to the object being handled, not the general mood.

### P07 / P24

- Why compression is risky: Both are safety-oriented. Compression can flatten them into “慎重で安全を見る人”.
- Key distinguishing lever: Retain the difference between environment safety check and repeated safety confirmation.
- Bad short line to avoid: 安全をちゃんと見る。
- Guardrail for recommended writing: Use either the hazard context or the repeat-confirmation context in the line.

### P18 / P31

- Why compression is risky: Both are spacing and social rhythm. Short copy can collapse them into “人との距離を調整する人”.
- Key distinguishing lever: Keep the scale: one-to-one spacing vs group / room / community spacing.
- Bad short line to avoid: 距離感がうまい人。
- Guardrail for recommended writing: Use “相手” for one-to-one and “場 / グループ / 空気” for community rhythm.

## Output Contract

### Global Share-Card Rules

- Share-card copy is one notch tighter than the teaser layer.
- Share-card copy must remain Japanese-native, screenshot-capable, and one-read legible.
- Share-card copy may compress the hook, but may not erase the persona boundary.
- No line may rely on mythology names to make sense.

- No line may sound like a survey, counseling, corporate ad copy, or an AI assistant.

- 心理テストっぽい診断語
- 相談・カウンセリング調
- 企業ブランド広告調

- 抽象化しすぎた共感コピー
- 冗長で説明型の一文
- ミーム頼みで古くなる言い回し

- Add context and texture, not new structural logic.
- If the share-card needs mythology to make sense, it is too weak.
