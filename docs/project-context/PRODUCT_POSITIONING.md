# YORISOU — Positioning

*(Package 20, 2026-07-15 · canonical)*

## One sentence

The gentle Japanese companion that understands you through a personality test and stays beside you on LINE — warm, honest, and never clinical.

## Against alternatives

| Alternative | Where it fails the user | YORISOU's difference |
|---|---|---|
| Entertainment personality quizzes | One-shot dopamine; forgets you; often fake precision | A relationship: result storytelling → LINE companionship → consented depth |
| Counseling / therapy apps | Right for clinical needs; heavy, stigmatized, costly for the everyday gap | Explicitly non-medical companionship for the "I'm fine, mostly" majority — and it says so honestly |
| Generic AI chatbots | Boundaryless, inconsistent persona, engagement-optimized | Structured arc, consent-first, emotional safety as a hard rule, human review on paid depth |
| Social media | Performance pressure, public exposure | Private, one-to-one, zero-pressure |

## Positioning boundaries (permanent)

- Never positioned as diagnosis, therapy, or treatment — including implicitly ("scientifically proven", clinical vocabulary, medical iconography are all rejected).
- Result taxonomy and scoring are product identity: **not casually changed**, contract-tested in the repo.
- The paid layer is depth-with-consent (detailed reports, human-reviewed), never paywalled manipulation of emotional need.
- LINE is a companionship channel, not a marketing blast channel.

## Status honesty

YORISOU is **live in production** (https://yorisou.online) with the verified 120-question runtime, operating in consented pilot/early operations per the `docs/` corpus — not yet a scaled commercial product. Claims of user counts or revenue have no place in product context.
