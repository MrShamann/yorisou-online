# YORISOU — UI/UX Creative Direction (permanent)

*(Package 20, 2026-07-15 · canonical. YORISOU's direction is its own — do not import Kakari's administrative-trust or Mirai Move's intelligence-display languages.)*

## The feeling

**Gentle emotional clarity.** Like a warm letter from someone who listened carefully — soft, unhurried, precise about feelings without being clinical about them. The product should feel handwritten-warm yet quietly well-made; the user's pulse should slow, not quicken.

## Emphasize

- **Japanese mobile reading** — typography, line length, and rhythm tuned for Japanese on phones; comfortable vertical flow; text that breathes (this is a reading product before it is an interface).
- **Progressive disclosure** — summary first, depth invited rather than dumped; the staged result reveal (skippable, server-rendered-first) is the canonical pattern.
- **Privacy and consent as felt qualities** — consent moments designed with the same warmth as results; the user always knows what is kept, why, and how to say no.
- **Human warmth without medicalization** — words like 寄り添う, feelings named plainly; never clinical charts, severity colors, or lab-report aesthetics.
- **Meaningful result storytelling** — results as narrative (who you are, told kindly and honestly) with visible limits (限界) and confidence in words; the trait constellation stays a *story image*, explicitly not a measurement.
- **Relationship continuity into LINE** — the transition to LINE is designed as "staying in touch", not "subscribe for updates"; LINE copy keeps the same voice.
- **Subtle motion** — soft, slow, respectful of `prefers-reduced-motion`; motion marks emotional beats (a reveal, a pause), never urgency.
- **Zero-pressure next actions** — every CTA has a graceful "not now"; declining is styled with equal dignity.

## Reject

- Clinical diagnosis aesthetics (scores as medical values, severity scales, condition language).
- Gamified addiction loops, streak pressure, scarcity timers, guilt re-engagement.
- Fake scientific precision (decimal-point personality numbers, "97% accurate").
- Coin/token mechanics of any kind.
- Generic chatbot-first interfaces — YORISOU is structured companionship, not an empty chat box.
- Automatic external messages (LINE or otherwise) without explicit product authorization.
- Dark manipulative conversion (hidden costs, pre-checked consents, shame copy).
