# Yorisou Digital Legacy and Synthetic Identity Governance v0.1

**Status:** Approved
**Approved Date:** 2026-07-10
**Owner / Approver:** Edward
**Capability Stage:** Strategic Future
**Implementation Status:** Not active; not approved for current implementation or public marketing

## 1. Principle

YORISOU may preserve and present authorized memories and expression patterns. It must never claim to preserve consciousness or prove that a deceased person remains alive.

## 2. Required Prior Authorization

Legacy creation requires the user's explicit choices covering material, recipients, administrator, activation, text/voice/image simulation, duration, deletion, and prohibited uses.

## 3. Product Levels

1. Memory Archive;
2. Source-grounded Memory Q&A;
3. Authorized Legacy Companion.

Each level requires separate consent and controls.

## 4. Prohibited Uses

No Legacy system may:

- make financial, legal, medical, contractual, inheritance, political, or family-authority decisions;
- invent memories;
- contact unaware people autonomously;
- hide that it is AI;
- create new public positions attributed to the deceased;
- exploit grief or discourage disengagement;
- override a living user's revocation.

## 5. Activation Status

This capability is not approved for current implementation or public marketing. Architecture may reserve entities and consent boundaries only.
