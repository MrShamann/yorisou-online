# YORISOU Revenue and Commercial Ethics v0.4.0

**Status:** Approved
**Pack:** YORISOU Governance Pack v0.4.0 · **Drafted:** 2026-07-13 · **Approved as activation candidate:** 2026-07-14 · **Approver:** Edward

## 1. Purpose
Govern how YORISOU earns money without corrupting the companionship promise.

## 2. Scope
All revenue products, pricing, and commercial mechanics.

## 3. Binding rules
1. Permitted revenue: test/report products (existing line, subordinated per Product Identity); future governed products under Phase G decisions.
2. **Memory consent is never monetized**: no paid tiers of consent, no data sale, no advertising against memory content, no paywall on deletion/correction/revocation — user controls are free forever.
3. Companion core features may be commercial someday ONLY via a Founder decision with an explicit dignity review; protective states and consent controls remain free regardless.
4. No recommendation may be undisclosed commercial placement.
5. Pricing communication is plain; no dark-pattern checkout, no fake urgency.

## 4. Prohibited behavior
Monetizing consent/controls; ads on memory; disguised placements; urgency mechanics.

## 5. Decision authority
Commercial terms: Edward only.

## 6. Required evidence
Revenue-product reviews at release gates; disclosure audits.

## 7. Exceptions
None on rule 2.

## 8. Change control
Change Management v1.0.
