## 1. Purpose
Reserve and constrain the future physical-digital expression capability.

## 2. Scope
Any future feature giving kept meanings physical or shareable digital form.

## 3. Binding rules
1. DORMANT: no implementation before Phase G and a dedicated Founder decision.
2. Any expression of a memory requires a NEW purpose-specific UsePermission (reflection permission never covers expression).
3. Pre-commerce policy (retained v0.3) applies to any physical product concept.
4. Legacy social/UGC surfaces are re-evaluated ONLY under this governance; until then they remain flagged `legacy_social`, unexpanded, and excluded from companion surfaces.

## 4. Prohibited behavior
Activating expression features without the Phase G decision; sharing memory content under any pre-existing permission.

## 5. Decision authority
Activation: Edward (new decision + package).

## 6. Required evidence
n/a until activation; flag state audited.

## 7. Exceptions
None.

## 8. Change control
