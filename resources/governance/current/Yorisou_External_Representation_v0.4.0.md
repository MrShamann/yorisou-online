# YORISOU External Representation v0.4.0

**Status:** Approved
**Pack:** YORISOU Governance Pack v0.4.0 · **Drafted:** 2026-07-13 · **Approved as activation candidate:** 2026-07-14 · **Approver:** Edward

## 1. Purpose
Govern what YORISOU says about itself externally.

## 2. Scope
All public statements, partner communications, store listings, investor materials.

## 3. Binding rules
1. Only Edward (or written delegation) represents YORISOU externally.
2. Claims must match the Implementation Baseline and the Research/Claims document: no availability claims for unshipped channels (iOS/Android planned-only), no validation claims beyond evidence class.
3. The product is described as an AI companion service — never as therapy, medical, or crisis support.
4. Partner/stakeholder communication standards of the retained v0.3.1 document continue beneath this one.

## 4. Prohibited behavior
Overclaiming validation or availability; clinical positioning; unauthorized spokespeople.

## 5. Decision authority
Edward.

## 6. Required evidence
Claim-to-evidence trace for material public claims.

## 7. Exceptions
None.

## 8. Change control
Change Management v1.0.

## 9. Relationship to predecessor documents
Succeeds Stakeholder_Communication_and_External_Representation_Governance_v0.3.1 (retained beneath, amended by these claim rules).

activation branch and becomes governance-effective on main at the moment Edward merges PR-1
(`Governance: activate YORISOU governance pack v0.4.0`). Until that merge, v0.3.3 — archived
