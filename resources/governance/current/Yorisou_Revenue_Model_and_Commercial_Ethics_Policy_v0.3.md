## 1. Purpose

This policy governs revenue generation without converting YORISOU into an attention-extraction or hidden-advertising platform.

## 2. Permitted Revenue Directions

Approved directions may include:

- paid deeper reports;
- premium private reflection and history features;
- clearly disclosed affiliate or referral income;
- paid curated collections or guided paths;
- business demand-intelligence products based on governed aggregation;
- concept-product validation and future partner services.

Each capability remains Planned until implemented and approved.

## 3. Prohibited Revenue Practices

YORISOU must not:

- sell identifiable user data;
- sell private test answers or private notes;
- target users through inferred vulnerability;
- disguise advertising as organic peer experience;
- charge for crisis access or basic safety controls;
- create artificial scarcity or manipulative countdowns;
- optimize infinite engagement to increase ad inventory;
- promise outcomes that cannot be substantiated.

## 4. Commercial Separation

User experience, editorial content, YORISOU-owned products, partner resources, affiliate items, and paid placement must be identifiable as different categories.

A commercial relationship must be disclosed before the user takes a consequential action.

## 5. Pricing Principles

Pricing must be understandable before purchase. Scope, delivery form, cancellation, refund, and limitations must be stated. Digital reports must be described as interpretive products, not professional determinations.

## 6. Community Integrity

Users must not be paid or rewarded for fabricated testimonials, fake helpfulness, mass posting, or promotional spam. Any future creator or contributor program requires separate governance.

## 7. Founder Approval

New revenue mechanisms involving personal data, user-generated content, targeted recommendation, payment, partner placement, or external data sharing require explicit founder approval before production release.
