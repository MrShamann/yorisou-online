## 1. Purpose

This policy governs early validation of possible products, services, collections, reports, or partner resources before they become purchasable offerings.

## 2. Capability Labels

Every concept must be labeled as one of:

- Idea;
- Researching;
- Prototype;
- Testing interest;
- Planned;
- Available.

Only Available items may be represented as purchasable or operational.

## 3. Permitted Validation

YORISOU may test:

- interest;
- save or waitlist behavior;
- preferred format;
- acceptable price range;
- problem relevance;
- willingness to receive updates.

A test must not imply inventory, partnership, delivery date, or guaranteed launch when none exists.

## 4. User Data

Interest signals may inform product development. They must not be silently converted into high-pressure sales targeting or shared with a third party without the appropriate basis and disclosure.

## 5. Social Content Separation

User experience cards must not be converted into product endorsements. Any quoted or featured experience requires appropriate permission and must preserve material context.
