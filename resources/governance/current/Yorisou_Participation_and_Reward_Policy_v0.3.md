## 1. Purpose

This policy governs voluntary participation in experience sharing, product feedback, testing, validation, research, and future contribution programs.

## 2. Ordinary Participation

Normal use does not require a reward. Users may voluntarily:

- complete tests;
- save or try recommendations;
- provide helpful/not-for-me feedback;
- publish an experience card;
- report content;
- answer optional product questions.

## 3. No Coercion

Access to basic privacy, deletion, reporting, account recovery, or ordinary product functions must not depend on completing promotional tasks or surrendering additional personal data.

## 4. Rewards

Possible rewards include:

- access to an additional reflection;
- early access to a feature;
- a discount or credit;
- acknowledgment within a governed contributor program;
- direct compensation for a defined research or content task.

Rewards must be described before participation. They must not encourage fabricated experiences, mass posting, or unsafe disclosure.

## 5. User-Generated Experience

Publishing an experience does not transfer ownership to YORISOU. The user grants only the license required to display and operate the content under the selected visibility conditions and terms.

## 6. Research and Special Programs

Any program that uses content beyond ordinary service operation—such as external research, partner validation, or paid case development—requires a separate explanation and consent path.

## 7. Young Users

YORISOU does not impose an adult-only product rule in v0.3. However, age-appropriate presentation, privacy protection, prohibited contact patterns, and legally required guardian or age handling must be addressed before features specifically target children or enable high-risk interaction.
