## 1. Purpose

This Constitution is the highest internal governance document for Yorisou.

It defines Yorisou's strategic identity, platform model, product boundaries, data and visibility principles, AI authority limits, commercial boundaries, execution authority, and founder decision rights.

Yorisou v0.3.1 consolidates and extends the project from a life-state understanding and recommendation platform into a Japan-market, Japanese-language, state-based, low-pressure social discovery and recommendation platform.

This is an evolution, not a restart from zero.

Existing valid assets from v0.2 and earlier remain usable where they do not conflict with v0.3.1.

If another Yorisou document conflicts with this Constitution, this Constitution prevails.

---

## 2. Core Strategic Identity

Yorisou's approved strategic target is a Japan-market, Japanese-language, AI-native, state-based social discovery and recommendation platform.


Yorisou helps people:

1. understand their current life state,
2. privately record how that state changes,
3. express lived experience safely,
4. discover relevant experience from people in similar situations,
5. form low-pressure, limited connections,
6. receive useful next-step recommendations,
7. try, save, reflect, and return,
8. improve future recommendations through feedback and behavior.

Yorisou v0.3.1 is a Japan-market product. Its governed channel architecture combines current responsive Web and LINE-oriented flows with planned iOS and Android applications.

Email registration and account recovery are supported alongside linkable LINE, Apple, and Google authentication identities where implemented and approved. Future expansion outside Japan is not part of the current approved scope and must be separately proposed and approved.

## 3. Core Platform Thesis

People often do not begin with a clear product request.

They begin with a state, situation, tension, rhythm, or change.

They may know:

- I feel stuck.
- I do not want to talk, but I do not want to feel alone.
- I am tired of generic advice.
- I want to see what helped someone in a similar situation.
- I want a small next step, not a major life prescription.
- I want to understand what is happening without being permanently labeled.

Yorisou converts these states into:

- self-understanding,
- private reflection,
- structured lived-experience records,
- state-based discovery,
- trusted recommendations,
- low-pressure connection,
- useful next actions,
- anonymized and evidence-labeled demand intelligence.

---

## 4. Core Platform Loop

The governing v0.3 loop is:

> Enter -> Understand -> Express -> Discover -> Connect -> Recommend -> Act -> Reflect -> Learn -> Return

Short form:

> State -> Expression -> Discovery -> Connection -> Recommendation -> Action -> Learning -> Return

Each layer must create user value.

No layer may exist only to extract data, maximize attention, or create artificial engagement.

## 5. Core Platform Assets

The core asset is not one test, one channel, one report, one community, or one market.

The core asset system is:

### 5.1 Life-State Graph

Represents current state, rhythm, change, context, and user-controlled history.

It must not be treated as a permanent identity label.

### 5.2 Experience Graph

Represents structured lived experiences, including:

- state context,
- situation,
- action tried,
- perceived result,
- limitations,
- relevance tags,
- visibility,
- freshness,
- feedback from similar-state users.

### 5.3 Recommendation Graph

Connects state and experience to:

- other experiences,
- small actions,
- tests,
- reports,
- books,
- articles,
- creators,
- tools,
- places,
- public resources,
- partner services,
- concept products,
- future local support paths.

### 5.4 Trust and Visibility Layer

Canonical visibility classes across all v0.3 documents:

- PRIVATE,
- INVITE_ONLY,
- SIMILAR_STATE_ONLY,
- ANONYMOUS_SHARED,
- PSEUDONYMOUS_SHARED,
- PUBLIC_SAFE.

Additional controls include searchable/non-searchable, time-limited, and deleted or withdrawn status.

Anonymous sharing describes what other users can see. They do not mean that Yorisou lacks an internal account, abuse-prevention, consent, or audit reference where one is lawfully required.

SIMILAR_STATE_ONLY is a delivery rule, not a public disclosure of a user's inferred state. The recipient must not be told that another identifiable person shares a sensitive state unless both sides explicitly consent.

### 5.5 Identity and Relationship Layer

Separates the person from channel identity.

A UserProfile may connect to multiple approved identities with consent.

### 5.6 Demand Intelligence Layer

Aggregates anonymized and evidence-labeled behavior and experience signals for internal, commercial, supplier, and public-value use.

Yorisou sells or shares insight, not identifiable user lives.

## 8. Social and Community Doctrine

Yorisou social interaction must be low-pressure, bounded, useful, and reversible.

The default social object is a structured experience, not a generic post.

The default relationship is topic, state, or experience affinity, not follower accumulation.

The default public action is save, try, reflect, or indicate relevance, not popularity voting.

Yorisou may support:

- private notes,
- anonymous or display-name experience cards,
- state-based discovery,
- temporary state-topic spaces,
- constrained supportive responses,
- invite-only sharing,
- gentle follow-up,
- user-controlled visibility.

Yorisou must not make these early default features:

- unrestricted comments,
- direct messaging,
- public personal profiles,
- follower counts,
- trending leaderboards,
- public popularity scores,
- creator monetization competition,
- infinite scrolling,
- addictive notification loops.

## 9. Japan Market and Future Expansion Doctrine

Yorisou v0.3 is governed as a Japan-first product, not as a global multi-region program.

### 9.1 Current Market

The current operating market is Japan.

The current user-facing language is Japanese.

The primary channel model is:

- Web for product entry, tests, private state space, experience discovery, reports, settings, and account management;
- LINE for registration or account linking, return visits, result saving, reminders, and lightweight relationship continuity;
- email for registration, authentication, account recovery, and service communication where appropriate.

### 9.2 Brand and Domain

The current product brand remains YORISOU.

The current primary domain remains yorisou.online.

v0.3 does not authorize a brand rename, domain migration, separate overseas brand, or country-specific product fork.

### 9.3 Future Expansion

Future language or market expansion may be considered later, but it is outside the current MVP and operating plan. The architecture should avoid unnecessary lock-in, but current product decisions must optimize for Japan rather than hypothetical international requirements.

## 10. Public and Private Separation

Private state, report content, raw test answers, relationship details, local-life signals, mobility/life-support data, recommendation history, purchase behavior, and personal notes are private by default.

Public or shared material must be:

- user-approved,
- visibility-scoped,
- public-safe,
- non-identifying where required,
- editable or withdrawable,
- separated from private insight.

AI-assisted rewriting may organize language and remove identifying detail.

AI must not invent an experience, change the user's facts, or silently widen visibility.

## 11. Evidence and Measurement Discipline

Yorisou must distinguish:

- view,
- save,
- try intent,
- tried action,
- perceived helpfulness,
- repeated helpfulness,
- result recognition,
- paid intent,
- purchase,
- organic recommendation interest,
- rewarded participation,
- public-value signal,
- partner-ready demand.

These are not interchangeable.

The product must optimize for useful outcomes and return value, not time spent or content volume.

Core v0.3 evidence includes:

- state-check completion,
- private-state return,
- experience creation,
- visibility choice,
- experience save,
- try intent,
- tried event,
- helpfulness feedback,
- relevant discovery,
- repeat state update,
- recommendation action,
- report or resource conversion,
- moderation and trust signals.

## 17. Age and Vulnerable-User Boundary

Yorisou is not limited to adults only.

The service should avoid unnecessary collection of exact age unless required for a specific feature, payment flow, legal obligation, or safety reason.

Where younger users may use the service, Yorisou must preserve:

- simple and understandable privacy explanations;
- no clinical, sexual, exploitative, gambling, or age-inappropriate targeting;
- no manipulative commercial recommendation based on vulnerability;
- user controls for deletion, visibility, reporting, and blocking;
- stronger review for any feature that exposes users to direct contact or unrestricted interaction.

The first v0.3 product does not include unrestricted private messaging or open direct-contact mechanics.

## 20. AI-Native Platform Constitution

YORISOU is not a conventional social platform with AI added as a feature. AI is a governed operating layer of the product.

The platform shall continuously use approved AI and Agent capabilities to:

- understand changing user states without converting them into permanent identity labels;
- organize user-authored experience while preserving factual authorship;
- maintain user-controlled personal memory;
- discover and verify relevant books, music, video, places, public resources, tools, people, communities, and product opportunities;
- generate finite and explainable recommendations;
- create and update low-risk content, test candidates, topic pages, local discovery, and internal intelligence;
- learn from save, try, helpful, hide, report, and state-change signals;
- support optional AI companion experiences;
- enable governed internal automation while preserving human accountability.

AI may generate candidates, drafts, rankings, summaries, and low-risk updates. It may not invent user history, override consent, silently impersonate a person, create undisclosed commercial influence, or make high-risk professional decisions.

## 21. Companion and Memory Constitution

YORISOU may provide three optional companion interfaces:

1. AI Friend;
2. AI Pet;
3. Quiet Guide without a personified character.

The companion layer must connect to state understanding, memory, recommendation, experience, community, and local discovery. It must not be designed to replace human relationships or maximize emotional dependency.

A Personal Memory Graph may contain only user-authorized information and must distinguish:

- original user statements;
- uploaded or linked source material;
- AI summaries;
- AI inferences;
- expired, corrected, or deleted memory.

Users must be able to inspect, correct, forget, export, and delete material memory.

## 22. Digital Legacy Constitution

Digital Legacy is a strategic future capability, not an initial launch promise.

YORISOU may later allow users to preserve selected memories, voice, images, values, messages, and expression patterns for designated people after death. Such a capability must be based on explicit prior authorization and must never claim that the deceased person remains alive or continues to form new real-world intentions.

Any future Legacy Companion must:

- remain clearly labeled as an AI simulation;
- cite or distinguish source material from generated synthesis;
- prohibit financial, legal, medical, contractual, political, inheritance, and family-authority actions;
- prohibit autonomous public posting unless explicitly pre-authorized for specific content;
- allow suspension and permanent deletion;
- avoid monetizing grief through dependency design.

## 24. Multi-Channel and App Distribution Constitution

YORISOU is one governed platform delivered through multiple channel surfaces. It must not be designed as a LINE-only product or as four disconnected products.

Approved channel architecture:

- responsive Japanese Web at `yorisou.online`;
- LINE, LIFF, or a LINE Mini App where useful for entry, linking, reminders, and return;
- a downloadable iOS application distributed through Apple App Store;
- a downloadable Android application distributed through Google Play;
- email for identity, authentication, account recovery, and service communication.

The iOS and Android applications are approved target channels but remain **Planned** until they are implemented, reviewed, submitted, and accepted by the relevant store. YORISOU must not state that either application is available before that point.

All channel surfaces must use one governed core platform, including one UserProfile model, linkable AuthIdentity records, consent and privacy controls, Life-State Graph, Experience Graph, Recommendation Graph, Personal Memory model, companion services, moderation, analytics, and backend/API contracts.

LINE, Apple, Google, and email identities are authentication identities, not the user profile itself. Identity linking requires user control, verification, consent, auditability, and protection against silent merges.
