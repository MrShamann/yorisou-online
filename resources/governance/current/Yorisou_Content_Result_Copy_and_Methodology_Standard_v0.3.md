## 1. Purpose

This standard governs Japanese user-facing copy for tests, state results, experience cards, recommendations, reports, trust pages, notifications, and social interactions.

## 2. Voice

YORISOU copy must be:

- calm and direct;
- natural in Japanese;
- emotionally precise without dramatization;
- respectful without excessive praise;
- non-clinical;
- non-deterministic;
- useful in small amounts.

Avoid corporate jargon, AI jargon, therapeutic performance, mystical certainty, and exaggerated reassurance.

## 3. State, Not Permanent Identity

Results describe a current pattern or tendency. Preferred structures include:

- 「今は〜に近い状態かもしれません」
- 「最近の回答では〜が見えています」
- 「今の自分に合うか、少し確かめてみてください」

Do not use language equivalent to “you are permanently this type.”

## 4. Test Universe

The content system may include:

- the 120-question deeper assessment;
- shorter current-state and practical checks;
- relationship and compatibility experiences;
- light symbolic or recurring reflection;
- fit and recommendation experiences.

No governance document may restore a fixed 31-persona assumption unless separately approved by the founder.

## 5. Experience Content

Experience cards should separate:

- situation;
- what was tried;
- perceived result;
- limits or conditions;
- who it may or may not fit;
- whether the text was AI-assisted.

Other users' responses should be low-pressure and bounded. Early-stage response mechanisms should favor structured reactions over unrestricted comment threads.

## 6. Sensitive Claims

Content must not imply diagnosis, treatment, guaranteed recovery, legal result, financial return, employment outcome, immigration result, relationship certainty, or public-service eligibility.

Where a user-generated experience contains such claims, it should be treated as one person's experience and not endorsed as platform fact.

## 7. Methodology Disclosure

Methodology pages should explain:

- what inputs are used;
- what the result can and cannot mean;
- that state can change;
- that recommendations are optional;
- that user feedback helps improve relevance;
- that YORISOU is not a professional authority.

## 8. Content Reuse

Private content is not reusable as public content. Anonymous or public-safe content may only be reused within the visibility and consent conditions selected by the author.
