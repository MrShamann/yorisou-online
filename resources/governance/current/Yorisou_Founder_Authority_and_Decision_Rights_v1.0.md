# YORISOU Founder Authority and Decision Rights v1.0

**Status:** Approved
**Pack:** YORISOU Governance Pack v0.4.0 · **Drafted:** 2026-07-13 · **Approved as activation candidate:** 2026-07-14 · **Approver:** Edward

## 1. Purpose
Fix which decisions are Edward's alone and how decisions are recorded.

## 2. Scope
All governance, production, commercial, research, and representation decisions.

## 3. Binding rules
1. Edward's sole, non-delegable rights: governance activation/amendment/archival; per-package production authorization; merge and release execution; kill-switch policy and manual rollback; research launch and participant contact; external validation/market claims; commercial terms; retention schedules; agent-role changes.
2. Delegable with written record: operational monitoring, evidence review, scheduled runtime operations within Agent Runtime governance.
3. Decision hygiene: every exercised right produces a decision record (register templates retained from v0.3.3); unsigned = not decided; silence is never approval.
4. Emergency rule: kill switch may fire before paperwork; the decision record follows the same day.

## 4. Prohibited behavior
Any agent exercising or presuming a Founder right; pre-filled or preselected decision documents; retroactive approval without a record.

## 5. Decision authority
Edward; amendments to this document are themselves a sole Founder right.

## 6. Required evidence
Decision records in the retained registers; activation/authorization signatures.

## 7. Exceptions
None.

## 8. Change control
Change Management v1.0.

## 9. Relationship to predecessor documents
Consolidates founder-authority clauses previously spread across Constitution v0.3.1 and the migration notes; archives Founder_Decision_Record_v0.3.3 as history.
