## 1. Purpose

This policy governs how YORISOU connects user state and experience signals to experiences, reflections, tests, reports, public resources, content, tools, places, and commercial resources in Japan.

## 2. Recommendation Order

The default priority is:

1. relevant user experience or YORISOU-owned guidance;
2. small, reversible action;
3. public or free resource;
4. non-commercial external content;
5. clearly disclosed commercial resource.

Commercial value must never override user relevance or safety.

## 3. Required Metadata

Every recommendable object must have, where applicable:

- object type;
- language and Japan applicability;
- source and ownership;
- commercial relationship status;
- last verified date;
- state/context tags;
- limitations;
- risk level;
- reason-for-recommendation template.

## 4. Matching Inputs

Permitted matching inputs include:

- user-selected state;
- test outcome and confidence;
- saved or tried content;
- helpful/not-for-me feedback;
- explicit topic interests;
- current session context;
- visibility-safe aggregate signals.

Private notes and private experience text must not be used for commercial targeting without separate, explicit permission.

## 5. User Controls

Users must be able to:

- see why something was recommended;
- hide an item;
- state that it does not fit;
- correct relevant profile signals;
- stop commercial recommendations where legally or product-operationally required;
- report misleading or unsafe recommendations.

## 6. No Deterministic Matching

YORISOU may say that an option may fit the user's current context. It must not claim certainty, guaranteed compatibility, destiny, diagnosis, or professional suitability.

## 7. Commercial Disclosure

Paid, affiliate, sponsored, referral, or partner items must be visibly disclosed before user action. Organic ranking and commercial ranking must be distinguishable internally and externally.

## 8. Quality Metrics

Primary quality measures are:

- relevance;
- save-to-try conversion;
- helpfulness after trying;
- low complaint and hide rates;
- explanation clarity;
- freshness;
- absence of undisclosed commercial bias.
