## 1. Purpose
Reserve and constrain life-continuity and legacy capabilities.

## 2. Scope
Any future feature concerning long-term continuity, inheritance, or post-life handling of kept meanings.

## 3. Binding rules
1. DORMANT: no implementation before Phase G and a dedicated Founder decision.
2. Retained bans from Digital Legacy & Synthetic Identity v0.1: no synthetic resurrection of persons; no posthumous chat simulation; no legacy feature without explicit, separate, revocable consent recorded in life.
3. Legacy directives, when built, attach to user + confirmed_memory with their own consent kind (entity reservation exists in the data model).
4. Deletion rights survive into legacy design: a deleted memory is deleted from legacy scope too.

## 4. Prohibited behavior
Synthetic identity of real persons; posthumous simulation; legacy defaults without explicit opt-in.
