## 1. Purpose

This policy governs statements to users, partners, government bodies, investors, media, suppliers, and the public.

## 2. Approved Current Description

YORISOU may currently be described as:

> A Japanese-language, state-based, low-pressure social discovery and recommendation product being developed for the Japan market through Web and LINE.

It may also state that it combines self-understanding, structured experience sharing, discovery, and optional recommendations.

## 3. Claims That Require Evidence

Do not claim without evidence that YORISOU:

- operates globally;
- has a large active community;
- has validated clinical or psychological effects;
- has active government, medical, commercial, or academic partnerships;
- has production capabilities that remain prototype or planned;
- represents Japanese society as a whole.

## 4. Brand and Domain

Current approved public brand and domain are:

- YORISOU;
- yorisou.online.

No rebrand, additional country brand, or global product name is authorized by v0.3.

## 5. Capability Language

Use explicit status labels: Current, Prototype, Planned, Deferred, or Researching. Avoid language that blurs intention and delivery.

## 6. Incident Communication

Material privacy, security, moderation, payment, or service incidents must be documented, escalated to the founder, and communicated consistently with legal and user-protection obligations.

## 7. Channel and App Availability Claims

Approved current description:

> YORISOU is a Japanese-language AI-native platform being developed for the Japan market, with a responsive Web product and LINE-oriented entry and return paths, and planned iOS and Android applications.

Until store acceptance is complete, external communication must use terms such as `planned`, `in development`, or `future app`. It must not display App Store or Google Play availability badges, download claims, review scores, or launch dates without evidence and approval.
