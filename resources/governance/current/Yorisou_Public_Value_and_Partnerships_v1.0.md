# YORISOU Public Value and Partnerships v1.0

**Status:** Approved
**Pack:** YORISOU Governance Pack v0.4.0 · **Drafted:** 2026-07-13 · **Approved as activation candidate:** 2026-07-14 · **Approver:** Edward

## 1. Purpose
Govern partnerships and public-value initiatives.

## 2. Scope
Any partnership, integration, institutional collaboration, or public-benefit program.

## 3. Binding rules
1. Partnerships never receive user personal context; integrations follow the no-connector rule of Consent Governance until amended.
2. Partner claims about YORISOU follow External Representation rules; YORISOU is never co-branded as a clinical service.
3. Public-value programs (e.g., digital-wellbeing education) must be evidence-honest and non-extractive.
4. Demand-intelligence and external-report boundaries of the retained v0.3 policy continue beneath this document.

## 4. Prohibited behavior
Data-sharing partnerships; clinical co-branding; pay-to-recommend arrangements.

## 5. Decision authority
Partnership approval: Edward.

## 6. Required evidence
Partnership records with data-flow statements (expected: none).

## 7. Exceptions
None on data rules.

## 8. Change control
Change Management v1.0.

## 9. Relationship to predecessor documents
New umbrella; Demand_Intelligence_and_External_Report_Policy_v0.3 retained beneath (amended).

activation branch and becomes governance-effective on main at the moment Edward merges PR-1
(`Governance: activate YORISOU governance pack v0.4.0`). Until that merge, v0.3.3 — archived
