# YORISOU Research, Validation and Marketing Claims v1.0

**Status:** Approved
**Pack:** YORISOU Governance Pack v0.4.0 · **Drafted:** 2026-07-13 · **Approved as activation candidate:** 2026-07-14 · **Approver:** Edward

## 1. Purpose
Bind the evidence-class vocabulary so claims can never outrun evidence.

## 2. Scope
All internal status language and all external marketing/research claims.

## 3. Binding rules
1. Evidence classes (ascending): SYNTHETIC_VALIDATION (autonomous system evidence — never described as human or market validation) → INTERNAL_HUMAN_REVIEW (Founder/dry-run) → MODERATED_HUMAN_RESEARCH (per the prepared research program, launch gated on Founder signature) → MARKET_EVIDENCE (real usage). Each claim must name its class.
2. The prepared moderated-research program (consolidated ops package) remains the only sanctioned human-research path; participant protections there are binding.
3. Marketing may describe design intent and synthetic-validation rigor honestly; it may not imply human outcomes without class-3+ evidence.
4. Research data handling follows the research program's minimization/separation rules; research claims cite gate results, not anecdotes.
5. 'Validated' unqualified is banned vocabulary; always qualify with the class.

## 4. Prohibited behavior
Class inflation; unqualified 'validated/proven'; implying therapeutic outcomes at any class.

## 5. Decision authority
Claim-class policy: Edward; per-claim sign-off for material public claims.

## 6. Required evidence
Claim register with evidence links for public materials.

## 7. Exceptions
None.

## 8. Change control
Change Management v1.0.
